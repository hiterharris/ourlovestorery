Sites

For Homestead Guests: There is a wide variety of recreational activities available including hiking, biking, kayaking / canoeing, outdoor pool, waterslide and lazy river, zip line, miniature golf, tennis, golf, serenity pool, spa and salon, game room and more. Advanced booking and appointments are required for certain activities. Please inquire with the hotel concierge.

Things to do at The Homestead

Hiking: There are numerous hiking and biking trails throughout Bath County. For a complete list, go to discoverbath.com, or ask for a trail map at The Homestead concierge desk.

Warm Springs Gallery: Be sure to visit this small but chic gallery and gift shop in Warm Springs

In addition to the activities and dining options above, more information can be found at DiscoverBath

Things to Know

Childcare: Homestead guests can contact the concierge desk for assistance

Grocery Store: Valley IGA Supermarket in Mitchelltown between Warm Springs and Hot Springs

Liquor Store: ABC Store (Virginia-speak for liquor store)
