Friday, September 7th

Welcome Party (all wedding guests)

Location: The Homestead Shooting Club, Hot Springs

Time: 7:00-9:00pm

What to wear: Dressy Casual (jacket and no tie for men & dress or dressy skirt for women)



A casual barbecue dinner will be served.

Bus transportation will be provided from The Homestead and The Inn at Gristmill Square, making regular trips between 6:45 and 9:15pm. (Sunset is at 7:40pm so we encourage everyone to arrive in a timely manner in order to enjoy the spectacular view over the Blue Ridge Mountains.)

In the event of rain, the Welcome Party will be held in the Commonwealth Room at The Homestead

Saturday, September 8th

Ceremony (all wedding guests)

Location: "Alpenglow" at Warm Springs Farm

Time: 5:00pm

What to wear: Cocktail Attire (jacket and tie for men & cocktail dress for women)
Please be sure to wear comfortable shoes for grass and dancing and bring a sweater in case it’s breezy. A pair of sunglasses wouldn’t be a bad idea either!


Van transportation will be provided from The Homestead and The Inn at Gristmill Square. Buses will depart promptly at 3:45pm from the front of The Homestead and 4:10pm from the front of The Inn at Gristmill Square

Cocktails, Seated Dinner and Dancing Reception to follow

Location: The Lodge at Warm Springs Farm

Sunday, September 9th

Brunch (all wedding guests)

Location: Snead's 1912 Steak, Hot Springs

Time: 9:30-11:30am

Snead's 1912 Steak is located across the street from the Homestead.
