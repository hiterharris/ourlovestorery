Welcome Party (all wedding guests)

Location: The Homestead Shooting Club

Time: 7:00-9:00pm

What to wear: Dressy Casual (jacket, no tie for men & dress or dressy skirt for women)



Food will be served. Bus transportation will be provided from The Homestead and The Inn at Gristmill Square, making regular trips between 6:45 and 9:00pm.
